# Course Overview
-----------------

* Learning Objectives
---------------------

In this course, we will:

. Review the role of ChatGPT in the high-tech industry.

. Explore the potential uses of ChatGPT in Education.

. Evaluate the benefits and potential challenges of using ChatGPT in government and law.

. Apply concepts of generative AI to practical scenarios in everyday life.

. Reflect on ethical considerations around using AI like ChatGPT.

. Predict how the workplace might evolve with the increasing integration of AI.

. Introduce the concept of artificial general intelligence (AGI).

. Consider hypotheses on how generative AI could evolve and impact society in the next 5-10 years.
